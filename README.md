# Olist Store-Data.Analysis (Interaction Dashboard creation using MS Excel,Tableau, Power BI, MYSQL)
## Project Objective
The Dataset is designed to help analysts and researchers better understand the e-commerce landscape in Brazil and identify opportunities for growth and optimization. To help Olist gain better insights into its e-commerce platform and optimize available opportunities for growth.

## Dataset used
- <a href="https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1fFe_5aEpHriuog5V7Fc9QOogpI_gdY4M">Olist Store Data</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
- Weekday Vs Weekend (order_purchase_timestamp) Payments Statistics.
- Number of Orders with Review score 5 and payments type as credit card.
- Average number of days taken for order_deliverered_customers date for pet shop.
- Average price and payments values from customers of Sao_paulo city.
- Relationship between shipping days (order_deliverered_customers_date_order_purchase_timestamp) Vs review scores.

## Insight
-Based on the data, Olist E-commerce processed approximately 99,442 orders, out of which around 89,940 were successfully delivered — indicating a 90% delivery success rate. The platform’s average product rating stands at 4.09 stars, with individual product categories ranging from 2.5 to 4.68 stars. Interestingly, one-star reviews rank third in frequency, suggesting potential issues related to product quality in certain categories. Delivery performance may also be influencing customer feedback, highlighting an opportunity to enhance logistics efficiency.
